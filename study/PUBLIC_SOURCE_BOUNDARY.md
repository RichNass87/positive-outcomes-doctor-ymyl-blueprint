# Public Source Boundary

## What this package proves

This package documents a public, versioned implementation concerning clinical-practice identity clarity, source provenance, and separation from unrelated colocated providers. The public archive is identified by DOI `10.5281/zenodo.21171669`.

## What it does not prove

This package does not establish medical efficacy, patient outcomes, clinical quality, legal compliance, credential validity, Google ranking, answer-engine ranking, platform endorsement, peer review, or Wikidata notability.

## Source classes

- **Independent institutional source:** UGA pages are suitable for Dr. Gayer's published academic/professional role only.
- **Canonical practice source:** Positive Outcomes is suitable for current practice facts subject to office review.
- **Conflict sources:** Healthgrades and other directories may reveal stale or blended information; they are not automatically authoritative.
- **Creator-controlled provenance family:** Zenodo, Academia, GitHub, Hugging Face, and the project website document public availability and version history. They do not count as independent reviews of the study.
